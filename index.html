The Twisted House

We are passionate about telling stories through video games.

We are The Twisted House, the new video game label born as the evolution of Nosolorol, Spain’s leading tabletop RPG publisher, with over 15 years in the business. Our team has created, published, and localized more than 500 titles, including IPs such as Vampire: The Masquerade, Adventure Time, Dune, and Blacksad. We’ve developed over 100 original role-playing products in six languages.

Now, we are leveraging all this narrative muscle and our ability to deliver ambitious projects on time and on budget to create a video game, Hell Hold’em, our debut title. In Hell, poker isn’t about good luck. It’s about how far you’re willing to cheat demons.

That’s Hell Hold’em, a tactical roguelike where every fight is a corrupted Poker duel against demons who cheat as much as you do. Swap, steal, burn, or duplicate cards. Every demon has its own style, and cheating is your only hope. Win, die, repeat until you cleanse your sins… or pile up new ones.
