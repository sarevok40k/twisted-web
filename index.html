About Us

In The Twisted House, we are passionate about telling stories through video games.

We are a studio dedicated to creating narrative-driven video games that transport players to fascinating worlds and immerse them in unforgettable experiences. Our mission is to bring quality stories to as many people as possible, combining our expertise in tabletop role-playing games with game design.

We believe that narrative is the true protagonist in a video game, and we strive to build immersive stories that stimulate the imagination and challenge players to make decisions that will affect the development and outcome of the plot.

We value the gaming experience above all else, designing non-linear and replayable video games, where each playthrough is a new opportunity. Additionally, we are committed to developing our games with low technical requirements to respect our environmental surroundings.

At The Twisted House, we are also committed to contributing to the growth and strengthening of the video game development industry in Spain, and to fostering the personal and professional development of our team. Join us and discover the magic of interactive narratives in each of our games.
