Myths are real.

Live adventures in Medieval Spain between magic and history.

Create a character and make decisions in a visual novel with RPG elements based on the acclaimed tabletop roleplaying game.

Aquelarre is an immersive, episodic visual novel with elements from RPG and point-and-click adventures based on the most famous Spanish tabletop roleplaying game. Create any character you like (a peasant, a warrior, even a noble!) and live in a version of Medieval Spain where myths come true.

Be part of different stories, make decisions, and face the unknown.

Stay tuned for the latest information and news about Aquelarre on this website and our The Twisted House profile on X.

Decide your birthright, choose your occupation, and live in the Dark Ages! Crimes, curses, and wars await. Your decisions will shape the story.

Develop your character through gameplay and forge your own destiny from the very start. Make choices that shape the narrative. Can you achieve the best outcome for each chapter?

Experience three unique stories crafted by some of the most renowned Spanish tabletop roleplaying game authors, including José Lomo and Juan Sixto.

Navigate a dark era where reality and myth blur together. Become part of the legends, uncover hidden mysteries, and encounter all the characters entwined in this adventure.

Immerse yourself in a unique type of visual novel, complete with minigames to tackle the toughest situations… or dive into «RPG mode» with dice rolls!

Back to the Dark Ages. Face the Devil. Join the Aquelarre.
